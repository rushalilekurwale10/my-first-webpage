Apps Of Online Shopping

  • Amazon: A widely trusted platform with a massive product range and fast delivery options.

  • Flipkard: A very popular app in India, offering a user-friendly interface and a huge selection of products across numerous categories.

  • Meesho: A popular app for budget-friendly and value-oriented shopping.

  • Myntra:Another major player, particularly for fashion and lifestyle products.

  • Nykaa:Specializes in beauty, wellness, and personal care products.

  • Shopsy: It is a social commerce platform launched by Flipkart that focuses on providing a zero-commission marketplace for sellers, especially those in smaller cities and towns, to reach a large base of budget-conscious customers.
